--- title: "PPTP VPN Configuration steps in Windows 7" category: "softwares" tags: ["installation"] language: "en" ---

To be able to use the VPN services that exist in the category Services there are a few things to note:

  1. You must already have internet connection.
  2. Make sure the port number 47 is open.

Step 1: Open Control Panel, Cick Start then click Control Panel

Step 2: Click Network and Internet

Step 3: Click Network and Sharing Center

Step 4: Click Set Up a Connection or Network

Step 5: Open Connect to a Workplace, Click Connect to a Workplace lalu Click Next

Step 6: Click Use my Internet connection

Step 7: Fill in the address obtained from the provider of the VPN Domain in the Internet address, and in the Destination name field with the name of the settings, so its will be easily recognized. To make sure the configuration can be saved, please check the Don't Connect now, just set it up so I can connect later. After completing click Next.

Step 8: Fill in User name and Password field with the username and password obtained from the VPN provider, and then click Create.

Step 9: To connect to the VPN click Icon Network Connection, click the name of the vpn connection has been previously named by you and then Click Connect. If it fail, Right Click the name of the vpn connections that have been named previously. Will Connect and Properties options appear, Click Properties.
